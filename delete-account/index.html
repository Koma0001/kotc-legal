King of the City — Account Deletion

You can request account deletion without reinstalling the app by emailing ktcspprt@gmail.com.

What to include in your request

  • Account email (the email used to sign in)
  • Username / display name (if applicable)
  • Optional: city/league or another identifier if duplicate names exist

What gets deleted

  • Supabase Auth account (email login)
  • Public profile (username/display name)
  • Challenge history and match records
  • Submitted video links (URLs). We do not store video files.

What may be retained

  • Minimal audit/security logs for fraud and abuse prevention (up to 30 days)
  • Short-lived backup copies until backup rotation completes

Processing timeline

We process deletion requests within 30 days.

Current monetization status

No in-app purchases and no ads in the current build.

Quick request form (email)

This form opens your email app with a pre-filled deletion request. If mailto does not work on your device/browser, send a manual email to ktcspprt@gmail.com.
